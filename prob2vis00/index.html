EXERCISE III

The Password
Complexity Logic

A scholar is tasked with creating a mechanism to judge the fortitude of a passphrase. One must discern between the brittle and the unbreakable by observing the following tenets of complexity:
  • 01.

    Very Weak

    Contains only numbers and is fewer than eight characters.

  • 02.

    Weak

    Contains only letters and is fewer than eight characters.

  • 03.

    Strong

    Contains letters and at least one number and is at least eight characters.

  • 04.

    Very Strong

    Contains letters, numbers, and special characters and is at least eight characters.
